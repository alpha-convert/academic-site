Joe's Restaurant List
This is a list of some of the restaurants (and a few bars) I've had the pleasure to visit while living in Philly. It's heavily inspired by Penn CIS Prof Lyle Ungar's legendary list of Philly restaurants, which is required reading for any food-loving new arrival to the city. I track all of my restaurant visits on Beli, if you want updates day-to-day for some reason.
