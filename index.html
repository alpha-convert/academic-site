Hi! I'm Joseph Cutler, but feel free to call me Joe. I'm a CS PhD student at UPenn, where I research type systems with Benjamin Pierce. My work is supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship, and I recieved an Honorable Mention for the 2025 Jane Street Graduate Research Fellowship.

I've interned at Nvidia (ML Compilers), Amazon Web Services (Cedar), and at Correct Computation, Inc.

Outside of PL, I run for Philadelphia Runner Track Club.
